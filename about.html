About Me

Hello! My name is Jack Xavier Sosa. I've been a student at Belen since sixth grade. I started taking STEAM courses in my eigth grade year, navigating through Robotics,then segueing to Introduction to Engineering Design, until I finally reached Honors Design and Innovation in my senior year. Ever since I was a little kid, I always enjoyed building cool things with LEGO bricks. This is where my interest in engineering was born. Now at Belen, I am able to take this passion and utilize it in the real world.

Outside of school, my favorite pastime is probably listening to music or hanging out with friends. I also used to enjoy learning and researching about computers, until I finally got my own that I use to play videogames with friends. I also run Track and Field, and go to the gym. Exercise really helps me destress and generally makes me feel better. On the weekends, I used to work at Publix Supermarkets. Here I would bag groceries, collect shopping carts, and sometimes even cashier. During the summer I worked at my school's summercamp. These two jobs helped make me more responsible and kept me busy.

Feel free to explore my work through this website. I'm excited to share my projects and the process behind them.
