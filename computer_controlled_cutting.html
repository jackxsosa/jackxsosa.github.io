Vinyl Cutter

In this section, I used Affinity design to create a vinyl sticker just for personal use. I made the Batman logo just becasue Batman is one of my favorite superheroes.

Step-by-Step Process

Below is a detailed step-by-step process of how I created the vinyl sticker.

Step 1: Outline

I started by importing an outline of the Batman symbol so that I can trace it to make the sticker look as accurate as possible.

Step 2: Trace

This was my sketch of the bat. I traced it, but there were some spots that didn't come out perfect. I decided to keep the imperfections and just continue on.

Step 3: Fill

Here, I filled in the bat to make it a solid black.

Step 4: Black Oval

Next, I made the bottom layer. I made a black oval as the bottom part of the sticker.

Step 5: Yellow Oval

I then made the second layer, a smaller yellow oval.

Step 6: Background

Here I put the yellow oval over the balck oval, creating the background of the sticker where the bat will be.

Step 7: Final Sticker

Finally, I put the bat into place, creating the final product.
